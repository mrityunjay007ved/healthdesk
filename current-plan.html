Current Workout Plan

Your personalized fitness journey with daily breakdown

Goals

Establish consistent workout habit.
Begin gentle cardiovascular conditioning (Zone 2).
Build mobility to offset travel stiffness.
Track baseline metrics (resting HR, HRV, weight, sleep quality).

Daily Breakdown

Monday – Strength & Core (Gym, 60 min)

Warm-up: 5 min treadmill walk @ brisk pace.

Circuit (3 rounds, 10–12 reps each, 60 sec rest between rounds):

  • Goblet Squat (dumbbell or kettlebell)
  • Push-ups (knee or full)
  • Seated Row (machine or resistance band)
  • Glute Bridge
  • Plank – 45 sec hold

Cool-down: 5 min full-body stretch.

Tuesday – Zone 2 Cardio (Outdoor or Treadmill, 30 min)

Maintain HR at 60–70% of max (Garmin monitor).

Brisk walk or light jog — can be done during travel.

Post-cardio: 5 min hip flexor & hamstring stretch.

Wednesday – Mobility & Strength (Travel-Friendly, 45 min)

Dynamic warm-up: arm circles, leg swings, torso twists.

Bodyweight circuit (4 rounds, 45 sec work, 15 sec rest):

  • Air Squats
  • Incline Push-ups (hotel desk/bench)
  • Reverse Lunges
  • Superman Hold
  • Side Plank – 30 sec/side

Finish with 10 min mobility flow (cat-cow, child's pose, spinal twist).

Thursday – Zone 2 Cardio + Short Intervals (35 min)

20 min steady pace walk/jog (HR 60–70% max).

Then 5 rounds:

  • 1 min faster pace run/walk
  • 1 min easy walk

Cool-down: 5 min light walking.

Friday – Strength & Core (Gym, 60 min)

Warm-up: 5 min rowing machine or brisk walk.

Circuit (3 rounds, 8–10 reps, moderate weight):

  • Dumbbell Deadlift
  • Overhead Press
  • Step-ups (bench or sturdy box)
  • Lat Pulldown (machine)
  • Side Plank Hip Lifts – 12 reps/side

Stretch: hamstrings, chest, shoulders.

Saturday – Light Cardio & Recovery (30 min)

20 min casual cycling, swimming, or brisk walk.

10 min full-body stretching / foam rolling.

Sunday – Full Rest

Optional: 15 min easy walk after meals.

Prioritize sleep and hydration.

Lifestyle & Tracking for Week 1

Sleep

Target 7–8 hrs, consistent bedtime/wake time.

Hydration

2.5–3 L/day (more on travel days).

Nutrition

  • Protein at every meal (eggs, chicken, fish, lentils).
  • Add 2 servings of Omega-3 rich food/week.
  • Avoid heavy dinners < 3 hrs before bed.

Tracking

  • Record daily steps, HR, HRV via Garmin.
  • Note energy levels & sleep quality (subjective score 1–10).
